Ryzen 3 3300X

Description

The AMD Ryzen 3 3300X is a high-performance quad-core processor designed for gamers and content creators on a budget. It delivers exceptional single-threaded and multi-threaded performance.

Specifications

  • Cores: 4
  • Threads: 8
  • Base Clock: 3.8 GHz
  • Boost Clock: 4.3 GHz
  • Cache: 18 MB
  • TDP: 65W
  • Socket: AM4
