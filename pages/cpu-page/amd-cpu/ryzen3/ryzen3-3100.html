Ryzen 3 3100

Description

The AMD Ryzen 3 3100 is a budget-friendly quad-core processor designed for entry-level gaming and multitasking. It offers excellent performance for its price point.

Specifications

  • Cores: 4
  • Threads: 8
  • Base Clock: 3.6 GHz
  • Boost Clock: 3.9 GHz
  • Cache: 18 MB
  • TDP: 65W
  • Socket: AM4
