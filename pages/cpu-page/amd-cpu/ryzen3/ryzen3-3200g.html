Ryzen 3 3200G

Description

The AMD Ryzen 3 3200G is a quad-core processor with integrated Radeon Vega 8 graphics, making it an excellent choice for budget gaming and general-purpose computing.

Specifications

  • Cores: 4
  • Threads: 4
  • Base Clock: 3.6 GHz
  • Boost Clock: 4.0 GHz
  • Cache: 6 MB
  • TDP: 65W
  • Socket: AM4
  • Integrated Graphics: Radeon Vega 8
